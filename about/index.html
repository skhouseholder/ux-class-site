About

The class

{{ site.class-title }} is built from the ground up for engineers, app developers, and startup founders. It focuses on one main theme: executing business goals.

The principles and methods you'll learn are universally applicable. Use your new skills to create great experiences on web, mobile, SAAS products, and even new-medium products like the Pebble watch or Nest thermostat.

The curriculum

After taking {{ site.class-title }}, you will be able to:

  • Optimize a landing page to maximize signups
  • Design attractive and usable pages and screens
  • Understand and utilize the fundamental principles of user-focused design
  • Conduct lightweight and heavyweight user research
  • Translate qualitative user feedback into useful quantitative metrics
  • Understand the important parts of typography
  • also lots more stuff!
